about us

Datatracker PTY - (LTD) is a business software service provider and specializes in developing high quality business software. We are here to provide our customer with a professional service. Our end goal is to provide streamlined software which adds business value to our customers.

OUR DEVELOPMENT APPROACH

With the speed at which technology is evolving, Datatracker has acknowledged the need to continuously evolve its thinking and keep its software up to date with technological advancements. This is why we have implemented an Agile Development approach to ensure we can always shift the goal posts and never get left behind.

Creative reliable software solutions are a top priority, we approach all projects with a team of experts, this helps us to push the limits and always strive for more.

We keep things simple to get you up and running in the minimum amount of time, keeping close in mind the evolvable needs of all our projects and maximizing delivery within your budget.



the process

KICK OFF MEETING

This meeting serves as an introduction between yourselves and your soon to be business partner. We use this meeting to get an understanding of your business needs, provide expert advice on achieving your goals and creating a plan for our future business interactions.

GATHERING YOUR REQUIREMENTS

During this phase, DataTracker will work closely with your business to determine all of its requirements. We analyse these requirements thoroughly and advise on the simplest and most effective approach to implement these requirements.

Once this phase has been completed DataTracker will provide a comprehensive document on its findings and suggested approach towards solving your needs. We consider this as the most important phase of development as it provides us and your business the opportunity to get a comprehensive understanding of our joint goals, a stepping stone to an effective partnership.

This enables us to formalise our development approach, set up time bound objectives, provide a development plan, consider your budgetary requirements and prioritise delivery.

DEFINING THE MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT (MVP)

It is important to understand that all products can be improved upon and the first release of any product is there to create market interest and provide profitable results.

We focus our attention on defining the minimal viable product and achieving that as quickly as possible, once this has been achieved we move our focus onto refinement and setting your product ahead of the competition.

BREAKING DOWN REQUIREMENTS INTO WORKABLE TASKS

In order to simplify the development process we analyse requirements and reduce them into a list of manageable tasks that can be achieved in a reasonable amount of time and easily measured for completion.

ALLOCATING TASKS INTO TIME BOUND SPRINTS

All our tasks are prioritised into time bound sprints which are released for testing once the sprint has been completed.

PROGRESSING TIME BOUND SPRINTS

Our developers prioritize tasks and select which high priority tasks they want to work on during a sprint. This maintains morale as well as fast tracks product development. Developers provide daily progress statuses on the selected tasks.

TESTING, ANALYSIS AND QUALITY ASSURENCE

All completed tasks undergo a code review process before they are accepted as complete and made available for customer testing and feedback.

ON COMPLETION OF MVP

Step 4,5,6,7 is an iterative process until MVP is reached. Once MVP is reached your first release is deployed.
